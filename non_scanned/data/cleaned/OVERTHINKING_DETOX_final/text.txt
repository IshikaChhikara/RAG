A Gentle Guide
This is not a diary. This is a release.
This planner does not require discipline, perfection,
or consistency.
You donʼt have to complete every page.
You donʼt have to follow any order.
You donʼt even have to open it every day.
Overthinking heals when pressure is removed.
If a page feels heavy, skip it.
If a question feels uncomfortable, leave it blank.
This planner works even when you donʼt.
When you reach the Mental Dump pages, choose
one situation or problem that is occupying your
mind right now.
Not your whole life. Not everything at once.
Just the one thought that keeps returning, even
when you try to distract yourself.
Write quickly. Write imperfectly.
Pour out anything related to that one issue —
thoughts, fears, questions, anger, confusion.
Stop the moment you feel lighter.
There is no wrong way to use this planner.
The only goal is to feel a little more space
inside your mind.
You are allowed to go slow.
